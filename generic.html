A Little Bit About Myself

Hello, my name is Guy McCroy. Thanks for visiting my digital portfolio. To me, it's more than just a place to demonstrate my competence using various programs and analytical methods. It's an online space designed so you can get to know me a little better.

The goal of my website is twofold. Firstly, to demonstrate that I can figure out the syntax needed to get things to work in various programs. That means although some online profiles contain more projects than others, to me, that is less important than showcasing my flexibility to get the result I wanted out of it. We live in a world of rapidly changing technology, so it's critical that we remain flexible with the programs we work with. For example, being more of a statistical programmer and less of a computer programmer, my kaggle profile with have more project examples than my GitHub because kaggle makes it easy to work with R and Python in the browser. However, Github hosts and holds the website you are on right now! By learning a little html and some GitHub basics, I avoided having to pay money to anyone to have an online portfolio.

Secondly, I wanted to my site to help demonstrate that I'm person with a range of interests who values learning new things. That's why you you'll find my goodreads account among the list of profiles on this site. Anything that demonstrates that I'm taking an active interest in something new will likely be represented somewhere on this site.
