A Little Bit About Myself

Hello, my name is Guy McCroy. Thanks for visiting my digital space. To me, it's more than just a digital portfolio. It's an online space designed so you can get to know me a little better.

The goal of my website is twofold. Firstly, to demonstrate that I can figure out the syntax needed to get things to work in various programs. That means although some online profiles contain more projects than others, to me, that is less important than showcasing my flexibility to get the result I wanted out of it. For example, being more of a statistical programmer and less of a computer programmer, my kaggle profile has more project examples than my GitHub because kaggle is my preferred place to work with R and Python in the browser. However, GitHub hosts and holds the website you are on right now! By learning a little html and some GitHub basics, I achieved my goal of hosting my site for free.

Secondly, I wanted to my site to help demonstrate that I'm person with a range of interests who values learning new things. That's why you you'll find my goodreads account among the list of profiles on this site. Any or all of my interests may one day take space on this site.
